Top three places to visit in Lagos

Civic Centre

The Civic Center is an office Space outlet and event hosting center. Its architecture is one of the most significant where it stands.

Victoria Island (V.I)

It is the main business and financial centre of Lagos. V.I is one of the most exclusive and expensive areas to reside in Lagos.

Dangote Refinery

The Dangote Refinery is an oil refinery owned by Dangote Group that was inaugurated on the 22nd of May 2023 in Lekki, Nigeria. When in full operation, it is expected to have the capacity to process about 650,000 barrels per day of crude oil, making it the largest single-train refinery in the world
